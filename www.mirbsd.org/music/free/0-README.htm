This subdirectory contains Free music under a licence approved for Free Cultural Works, by the Open Knowledge Foundation and/or Open Source Initiative, and sometimes even by the Ⓕ Copyfree Initiative (which does not permit copyleft licences) as well as music in the Public Domain.

The music is usually published (when protection applies) under Ⓕ The MirOS Licence (however see the individual files for details) and produced with the Free fonts from the resources directory and mirabilos’ MuseScore 2 style sheets for Free (vocal) pieces.
